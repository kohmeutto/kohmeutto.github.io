1) time scaling

$$ \mathcal{L}\left\lbrace f\left(at-b\right)u\left(at-b\right)\right\rbrace\left(s\right)=\langle s|f\left(at-b\right)u\left(at-b\right)\rangle=\frac{1}{a} e^{-\frac{b}{a}s}\left\langle \frac{s}{a} \middle|f\right\rangle $$$$ \text{ë‹¨, } a>0, b>0 $$

$$ \mathcal{L}\left\lbrace h\left(t\right)u(t)\ast f\left(t\right)u(t)\right\rbrace\left(s\right)=\langle s|h\cdot u\ast f\cdot u\rangle=\langle s|h\cdot u\rangle\langle s|f\cdot u\rangle $$
