(b) exponential - IOPM

1. $\frac{1}{f\left(D\right)}e^{\alpha x}=\frac{1}{f\left(\alpha\right)}e^{\alpha x,}, \quad f\left(\alpha\right)\ne0$

example1)

$$y''+y'-2y=e^{3x}$$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D+2\right)\left(D-1\right)y_{p}=e^{3x} $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D+2\right)^{}\left(D-1\right)\left|_{D=3}^{}\right.}_{}e^{3x}=\frac{1}{10}e^{3x} $$

example2)

$$ y''-y'-6y=e^{-x} $$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D-3\right)\left(D+2\right)y_{p}=e^{-x} $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D-3\right)^{}\left(D+2\right)\left|_{D=-1}^{}\right.}_{}e^{-x}=-\frac14e^{-x} $$

example3) @Seungmin Son

$$ y''-4y^{\prime}+3y=10e^{-2x} $$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D-3\right)\left(D-1\right)y_{p}=10e^{-2x} $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D-3\right)^{}\left(D-1\right)\left|_{D=-2}^{}\right.}10_{}e^{-2x}=\frac23e^{-2x} $$

example4) @Seungmin Son

$$ y''+4y^{\prime}+3y=-5e^{5x} $$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D+1\right)\left(D+3\right)y_{p}=-5e^{5x} $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D+1\right)^{}\left(D+3\right)\left|_{D=5}^{}\right.}_{}-5e^{5x}=-\frac{5}{48}e^{5x} $$

example5)

$$ x^2y''-4xy'+6y=2e^{4x} $$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D-3\right)\left(D-2\right)y_{p}=2e^{4x} $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D-3\right)^{}\left(D-2\right)\left|_{D=4}^{}\right.}2_{}e^{4x}=e^{4x} $$

example6) @Seungmin Son, 코시-오일러 비제차 문제를 만들 것.

$$ x^2y^{\prime\prime}-xy^{\prime}-8y=9e^{-7x} $$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D-4\right)\left(D+2\right)y_{p}=9e^{-7x} $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D-4\right)^{}\left(D+2\right)\left|_{D=-7}^{}\right.}9_{}e^{-7x}=-\frac{9}{55}e^{-7x} $$

2. ${\displaystyle\frac{1}{\left(D-\alpha\right)^{n}}e^{\alpha x}=\frac{x^{n}}{n!}e^{\alpha x}}$

example1)

$$ y''+2y'+y=e^{-x} $$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D+1\right)^2y_{p}=e^{-x} $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D+1\right)^2}_{}e^{-x}=\frac{x^2}{2}e^{-x} $$

example2)

$$ y''-6y'+9y=-e^{3x} $$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D-3\right)^2y_{p}=-e^{3x} $$$$ y_{p}=-\frac{1}{\left(D-3\right)^2}_{}e^{3x}=-\frac{x^2}{2}e^{3x} $$

example3) @Seungmin Son

$$ y''+4y'+4y=3e^{-2x} $$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D+2\right)^2y_{p}=3e^{-2x} $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D+2\right)^2}_{}3e^{-2x}=\frac{3x^2}{2}e^{-2x} $$

3. ${{\displaystyle\frac{1}{f\left(D\right)\left(D-\alpha\right)^{n}}e^{\alpha x}=\frac{x^{n}}{f\left(\alpha\right)n!}e^{\alpha x}}}$

example1)

$$ y''+y'-2y=e^{x} $$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D+2\right)\left(D-1\right)y_{p}=e^{x} $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left.\left(D+2\right)\right|_{D=1}\left(D-1\right)^{}}e^{x}=\frac13\cdot xe^{-x} $$

example2)

$$ y''-4y'+3y=-2e^{3x} $$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D-1\right)\left(D-3\right)y_{p}=-2e^{3x} $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left.\left(D-1\right)\right|_{D=3}\left(D-3\right)^{}}-2e^{3x}=\frac12\cdot x\cdot-2e^{3x} $$

example3)

$$ x^2y''-4xy'+6y=2e^{3x} $$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D-3\right)\left(D-2\right)y_{p}=2e^{3x} $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D-2\right)\left|_{D=3}^{}\right.\left(D-3\right)^{}}2_{}e^{3x}=2xe^{3x} $$

example4) @Seungmin Son , 코시-오일러 비제차 문제를 만들 것.

$$ x^2y^{\prime\prime}-4xy^{\prime}-14y=3e^{-2x} $$

  • sol)

    $$ \left(D+2\right)\left(D-7\right)y_{p}=3e^{-2x} $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D-7\right)\left|_{D=-2}^{}\right.\left(D+2\right)^{}}3_{}e^{-2x}=-\frac{x}{3}e^{-2x} $$
