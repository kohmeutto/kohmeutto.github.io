(b) ODE - Homogenous

1. 상미분제차차방정식

$$ \begin{aligned} &y''+ay'+by=0 \\ \\ &D^2+aD+b=0 \end{aligned} $$

2. 서로 다른, 실수 해

$$ \begin{aligned} &D=\alpha,\beta \\ \\ &y=c_1e^{\alpha x}+c_2e^{\beta x} \end{aligned} $$

example1)

$$y''-y'-2y'=0$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D-2\right)\left(D+1\right)=0\rightarrow D=-1,2 \\ \\ &y=c_1e^{-1x}+c_2e^{2x} \end{aligned} $$

example2)

$$y''-4y'=0$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &D\left(D-4\right)=0\rightarrow D=0,4 \\ \\ &y=c_1+c_2e^{4x} \end{aligned} $$

2. 중근

$$ \begin{aligned} &D=\alpha \\ \\ &y=\left(c_1+c_2x\right)e^{\alpha x} \end{aligned} $$

example1)

$$y''+6y'+9y=0$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D+3\right)^2=0 \rightarrow D=-3 \\ \\ &y=\left(c_1+c_2x\right)e^{-3x} \end{aligned} $$

3. 서로 다른, 복소수 해

$$ \begin{aligned} &D=\alpha\pm i\beta \\ \\ &y=e^{\alpha x}\left(c_1\cos\beta x+c_2\sin\beta x\right) \\ \\ &y=e^{\alpha x}\left(c_1e^{+i\beta x}+c_2e^{-i\beta x}\right) \end{aligned} $$

example1)

$$y''+4y=0$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &D^2+4=0\rightarrow D=\pm 2i \\ \\ &y=c_1\cos 2x+c_2\sin 2x \end{aligned} $$

example2)

$$y''+2y'+2y=0$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &D^2+2D+2=0\rightarrow D=-1\pm \sqrt{1-2}=-1\pm i \\ \\ &y=e^{-x}\left(c_1\cos x+c_2\sin x\right) \end{aligned} $$

example3)

$$y''-2y'+10y=0$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &D^2-2D+10=0\rightarrow D=1\pm \sqrt{1-10}=-1\pm 3i \\ \\ &y=e^{x}\left(c_1\cos 3x+c_2\sin 3x\right) \end{aligned} $$

example4)

$$y'''-2y''+3y'-6y=0$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &D^3-2D^2+4D-6=0\rightarrow \left(D-2\right)\left(D^2+3\right)=0 \\ \\ &y=c_1e^{2x}+c_2\cos 3x+c_3\sin 3x \end{aligned} $$

example5)

$$y'''-6y''+12y'-8y=0$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &D^3-6D^2+12D-8=0\rightarrow \left(D-2\right)^3=0 \\ \\ &y=e^{2x}\left(c_1+c_2x+c_3 x^2\right) \end{aligned} $$
