(p) ODE solution using GF

  • *Matrix operator - Inner product

0. 정리

$$ Lu=f\left(x\right) $$

위 연산에서,

$$ u=u_{h}+u_{p},\quad u_{h}: \text{일반해},\quad u_{p}: \text{특수해} $$$$ Lu_{h}=0 $$$$ Lu_{p}=f\left(x\right) $$

특수해에 대해서 그린함수를 사용한다.

  • -uriencoded–LG\left(x,x%5e%7B\prime%7D)=\delta(x-x%5e%7B\prime%7D\right)
  • -uriencoded–L\int G\left(x,x%5e%7B\prime%7D\right)f\left(x%5e%7B\prime%7D\right)dx%5e%7B\prime%7D=\int\delta\left(x-x%5e%7B\prime%7D\right)f\left(x%5e%7B\prime%7D\right)dx%5e%7B\prime%7D
$$ u_{p}\left(x\right)=\int_{\Omega^{\prime}}G\left(x,x^{\prime}\right)f\left(x^{\prime}\right)dx^{\prime} $$
	**그린함수의 boundary condition 0이 되도록 설정** (다른 것도 가능하나) 하는 것이 편하다.

일반화하면, 다음과 같다.

$$ u_{p}\left(\bm{r}\right)=\int_{\Omega^{\prime}}G\left(\bm{r},\bm{r}^{\prime}\right)f\left(\bm{r}^{\prime}\right)d\Omega^{\prime} $$

1. 해의 이해

$$ \bm{r}:\text{ 관측하고자 하는 위치에서의 벡터, 관측벡터} $$$$ \bm{r}^{\prime}:\text{ 함수 f가 0이 아닌 값이 정의되어 있는 영역에서의 벡터} $$$$ G\left(\bm{r},\bm{r}^{\prime}\right)f\left(\bm{r}^{\prime}\right):\text{r'의 f가 관측위치 r에 어떻게 영향을 주는지 나타낸다.} $$

따라서,

$$ \begin{align}& u\left(\bm{r}\right)=\int_{\Omega^{\prime}}G\left(\bm{r},\bm{r}^{\prime}\right)f\left(\bm{r}^{\prime}\right)d\Omega^{\prime}\\&:\text{모든 r'의 f 가 관측위치 r에 어떻게 영향을 주는지를 나타낸다.} \end{align} $$

example1)

Find the general solution of the given differential equations. Don’t evaluate the integral that defines y$_{p}$(x).

(1) $y’’-16y=xe^{-2x}$ (단, x의 범위는 -∞…∞)

  • sol, IOPM-D

    (a) IOPM - displacement

    $$ D^2-16=0 $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D+4\right)\left(D-4\right)}\left\lbrack xe^{-2x}\right\rbrack=\frac{e^{-2x}}{\left(D+2\right)\left(D-6\right)}\left\lbrack x\right\rbrack $$$$ =\frac{e^{-2x}}{D^2-4D-12}\left\lbrack x\right\rbrack=-\frac{e^{-2x}}{12}\cdot\left\lbrace1-\frac13D+\frac{1}{12}D^2\right\rbrace\left\lbrack x\right\rbrack=-\frac{e^{-2x}}{12}\left(x-\frac13\right) $$

  • sol, GF

    1. $x\ne x'$
    $$ \left(D^2-16\right)G\left(x,x^{\prime}\right)=0 $$

    경계조건에서 0이어야 하므로,

    $$ \begin{aligned}G\left(x,x^{\prime}\right)=\begin{cases}c_1\left(x^{\prime}\right)e^{4x} & xx^{\prime}\end{cases}\end{aligned} $$
    1. $x=x^{\prime}$
    $$ \begin{aligned} W=\left|\left[\begin{matrix}e^{4x} & e^{-4x}\\ 4e^{4x} & -4e^{-4x}\end{matrix}\right]\right|=-4-4=-8 \end{aligned} $$$$ c_1\begin{aligned}=-\frac{e^{-4x^{\prime}}}{8}\end{aligned},c_2\begin{aligned}=-\frac{e^{4x^{\prime}}}{8}\end{aligned} $$
    1. y$_{p}$(x)

    $$ y_{p}=-\frac18\int_{x}^{\infty}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack e^{-4x^{\prime}}e^{4x}\cdot x^{\prime}e^{-2x^{\prime}}\right\rbrack-\frac18\int_{-\infty}^{x}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack e^{4x^{\prime}}e^{-4x}\cdot x^{\prime}e^{-2x^{\prime}}\right\rbrack $$$$ =-\frac{e^{4x}}{8}\int_{x}^{\infty}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack x^{\prime}e^{-6x^{\prime}}\right\rbrack-\frac{e^{-4x}}{8}\int_{-\infty}^{x}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack x^{\prime}e^{2x^{\prime}}\right\rbrack $$

    문제에서는 전개하지 않아도 된다 했으나, 위의 결과와 비교해 본다.

    $$ \int_{x}^{\infty}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack x^{\prime}e^{-6x^{\prime}}\right\rbrack=\left\lbrack-\frac16x^{\prime}e^{-6x^{\prime}}-\frac{1}{36}e^{-6x^{\prime}}\right\rbrack\right|_{x}^{\infty}=\frac16xe^{-6x}+\frac{1}{36}e^{-6x} $$$$ \int_{-\infty}^{x}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack x^{\prime}e^{2x^{\prime}}\right\rbrack=\left\lbrack\frac12x^{\prime}e^{2x^{\prime}}-\frac14e^{2x^{\prime}}\right\rbrack\right|_{-\infty}^{x}=\frac12xe^{2x}-\frac14e^{2x} $$$$ y_{p}=-\frac18\left\lbrack\frac16xe^{-2x}+\frac{1}{36}e^{-2x}+\frac12xe^{-2x}-\frac14e^{-2x}\right\rbrack $$$$ =-\frac18\left\lbrack\frac23xe^{-2x}-\frac29e^{-2x}\right\rbrack=-\frac{e^{-2x}}{12}\left(x-\frac13\right) $$

example2) 중요

Solve the initial-value problem

$$ y''+4y=\sin2x,\quad y\left(0\right)=1,\quad y^{\prime}\left(0\right)=-2 $$

  • sol, IOPM-T

    특수해

    $$ D^2+4=0 $$$$ y_{p}=\frac{1}{D^2+4}\operatorname{\operatorname{Im}}\left\lbrack e^{j2x}\right\rbrack=\frac{1}{\left(D-j2\right)\left(D+2j\right)}\operatorname{\operatorname{Im}}\left\lbrack e^{j2x}\right\rbrack $$$$ =\operatorname{\operatorname{Im}}\left\lbrack\frac{x}{4j}\left(\cos2x+j\sin2x\right)\right\rbrack=-\frac{x}{4}\cos2x $$

    일반해

    $$ y_{h}=c_1\cos2x+c_2\sin2x $$

    최종해

    $$ c_1=1 $$$$ 2c_2-\frac14=-2\to c_2=-\frac78 $$$$ y_{}=\cos2x-\frac78\sin2x-\frac{x}{4}\cos2x $$

  • sol, GF

    • 특수해

    (1) $x\ne x'$

    $$ \left(D^2+4\right)G\left(x,x^{\prime}\right)=0 $$$$ \begin{aligned}G\left(x,x^{\prime}\right)=\begin{cases}0 & xx^{\prime}\end{cases}\end{aligned} $$

    (2) $x=x^{\prime}$

    continuous

    $$ \begin{aligned}G\left(x,x^{\prime}\right)=\begin{cases}0 & xx^{\prime}\end{cases}\end{aligned} $$

    jump condition

    $$ c_2\left(x^{\prime}\right)=\frac12 $$

    (3) y$_{p}$(x)

    $$ y_{p}=\int_0^{x}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack\frac12\sin2\left(x-x^{\prime}\right)\cdot\sin2x^{\prime}\right\rbrack+\int_{x}^{2\pi}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack0\cdot\sin2x^{\prime}\right\rbrack $$$$ =\frac12\int_0^{x}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack-\frac12\cos2x+\frac12\cos2\left(x-2x^{\prime}\right)\right\rbrack $$$$ =-\frac{x}{4}\cos2x+\frac14\int_0^{x}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack\cos2\left(x-2x^{\prime}\right)\right\rbrack $$$$ =-\frac{x}{4}\cos2x-\frac{1}{16}\left.\sin2\left(x-2x^{\prime}\right)\right|_{x^{\prime}=0}^{x}=-\frac{x}{4}\cos2x-\frac{1}{16}\left(\sin2\left(-x\right)-\sin2x\right) $$$$ =-\frac{x}{4}\cos2x+\frac18\sin2x $$
    • 일반해
    $$ y_{h}=c_1\cos2x+c_2\sin2x $$

    boundary condition 적용한다.

    $$ c_1=1 $$$$ 2c_2=-2\to c_2=-1 $$$$ y_{h}=_{}\cos2x-\sin2x $$
    • 최종해

example3) 매우중요

Solve the initial-value problem

$$ y''+4y=f\left(x\right),\quad y\left(0\right)=1,\quad y^{\prime}\left(0\right)=-2 $$

where the forcing function f is piecewise defined:

$$ \begin{aligned}f\left(x\right)=\begin{cases}0 & x<0\\ \sin2x & 0\le x\le2\pi\\ 0 & x>2\pi\end{cases}\end{aligned} $$

  • sol, GF

    $$ \begin{aligned}G\left(x,x^{\prime}\right)=\begin{cases}0 & xx^{\prime}\end{cases}\end{aligned} $$
    1. $x<0$

    $$ y_{p}=\int_0^{2\pi}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack0\cdot\sin2x^{\prime}\right\rbrack=0 $$

    적분범위를 유심히 살펴본다.

    1. $0\le x\le2\pi$

    $$ y_{p}=\int_0^{x}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack\frac12\sin2\left(x-x^{\prime}\right)\cdot\sin2x^{\prime}\right\rbrack+\int_{x}^{2\pi}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack0\cdot\sin2x^{\prime}\right\rbrack $$$$ =-\frac{x}{4}\cos2x+\frac18\sin2x $$
    1. $2\pi<x$

    $$ y_{p}=\int_0^{2\pi}dx^{\prime}\left\lbrack\frac12\sin2\left(x-x^{\prime}\right)\cdot\sin2x^{\prime}\right\rbrack $$$$ =-\frac{2\pi}{4}\cos4\pi+\frac18\sin4\pi=-\frac{\pi}{2} $$

    적분범위를 유심히 살펴본다.

example5)

Solve the boundary-value problem

$$ x^2y''-3xy'+3y=24x^5,\quad y\left(1\right)=0,\quad y\left(2\right)=0 $$

  • sol, GF

    스트룸-리우빌 형태로 변형한다.

    $$ \left\lbrack\left(D^2-\frac{3}{x}D\right)+\frac{3}{x^2}\right\rbrack y=24x^3 $$$$ r\left(x\right)=\exp\left\lbrace\int dx\left\lbrack\frac{3}{x}\right\rbrack\right\rbrace=x^3 $$$$ \left\lbrack D\left(x^3D\right)+3x\right\rbrack y=24x^6 $$
    1. $x\ne x'$

    특성방정식은 아래와 같다.

    $$ \left(\lambda-3\right)\left(\lambda-1\right)=0 $$$$ \begin{aligned}G\left(x,x^{\prime}\right)=\begin{cases}c_1\left(x^{\prime}\right)\left(ax^3+x\right) & xx^{\prime}\end{cases}\end{aligned} $$$$ a=-1,b=-4 $$
    1. $x=x^{\prime}$
    $$ \begin{aligned}W=\left|\left[\begin{matrix}-x^3+x & x^3-4x\\ -3x^2+1 & 3x^2-4\end{matrix}\right]\right|=-6x^3\end{aligned} $$$$ c_1\begin{aligned}=\frac{x^{\prime3}-4x^{\prime}}{-6x^{\prime6}}=-\frac{x^{\prime2}-4}{6x^{\prime}^5},\quad\end{aligned}c_2\begin{aligned}=\frac{-x^{\prime3}+x^{\prime}}{-6x^{\prime6}}=\frac{x^{\prime2}-1}{6x^{\prime5}}\end{aligned} $$$$ \begin{aligned} G\left(x,x^{\prime}\right)=\begin{cases}\displaystyle\frac{x^{\prime2}-4}{6x^{\prime}^5}\left(x^3-x\right) & xx^{\prime}\end{cases}\end{aligned} $$
    1. y$_{p}$(x)
    $$ y_p(x) = 6(4x - x^3) (\ln |x^4 - 4| - \ln 3) + 6(x - x^3) (16 - x^4) $$
