(b) exponential - IOPM

미분방정식의 해는 일반해+특수해 이다.

아래 예제의 풀이는 일반해를 생략하고, 특수해만 구하는 것이다.

1.

$$\frac{1}{f\left(D\right)}e^{\alpha x}=\frac{1}{f\left(\alpha\right)}e^{\alpha x,}, \quad f\left(\alpha\right)\ne0$$

example1)

$$y''+y'-2y=e^{3x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D+2\right)\left(D-1\right)y_{p}=e^{3x} \\ \\ &y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D+2\right)\left(D-1\right)\left|_{D=3}\right.}e^{3x}=\frac{1}{10}e^{3x} \end{aligned} $$

example2)

$$y''-y'-6y=e^{-x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D-3\right)\left(D+2\right)y_{p}=e^{-x} \\ \\ &y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D-3\right)\left(D+2\right)\left|_{D=-1}^{}\right.}e^{-x}=-\frac14e^{-x} \end{aligned} $$

example3)

$$y''-4y^{\prime}+3y=10e^{-2x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D-3\right)\left(D-1\right)y_{p}=10e^{-2x} \\ \\ &y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D-3\right)\left(D-1\right)\left|_{D=-2}\right.}10_{}e^{-2x}=\frac23e^{-2x} \end{aligned} $$

example4)

$$y''+4y^{\prime}+3y=-5e^{5x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D+1\right)\left(D+3\right)y_{p}=-5e^{5x} \\ \\ &y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D+1\right)\left(D+3\right)\left|_{D=5}\right.}-5e^{5x}=-\frac{5}{48}e^{5x} \end{aligned} $$

example5)

$$x^2y''-4xy'+6y=2e^{4x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D-3\right)\left(D-2\right)y_{p}=2e^{4x} \\ \\ & y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D-3\right)\left(D-2\right)\left|_{D=4}\right.}2e^{4x}=e^{4x} \end{aligned} $$

example6)

$$x^2y''-xy'-8y=9e^{-7x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D-4\right)\left(D+2\right)y_{p}=9e^{-7x} \\ \\ &y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D-4\right)\left(D+2\right)\left|_{D=-7}\right.}9e^{-7x}=-\frac{9}{55}e^{-7x} \end{aligned} $$

2.

$$\frac{1}{\left(D-\alpha\right)^{n}}e^{\alpha x}=\frac{x^{n}}{n!}e^{\alpha x}$$

example1)

$$y''+2y'+y=e^{-x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D+1\right)^2y_{p}=e^{-x} \\ \\ &y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D+1\right)^2}e^{-x}=\frac{x^2}{2}e^{-x} \end{aligned} $$

example2)

$$y''-6y'+9y=-e^{3x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D-3\right)^2y_{p}=-e^{3x} \\ \\ &y_{p}=-\frac{1}{\left(D-3\right)^2}e^{3x}=-\frac{x^2}{2}e^{3x} \end{aligned} $$

example3)

$$y''+4y'+4y=3e^{-2x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D+2\right)^2y_{p}=3e^{-2x} \\ \\ &y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D+2\right)^2}3e^{-2x}=\frac{3x^2}{2}e^{-2x} \end{aligned} $$

3.

$$\frac{1}{f\left(D\right)\left(D-\alpha\right)^{n}}e^{\alpha x}=\frac{x^{n}}{f\left(\alpha\right)n!}e^{\alpha x}$$

example1)

$$y''+y'-2y=e^{x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D+2\right)\left(D-1\right)y_{p}=e^{x} \\ \\ &y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left.\left(D+2\right)\right|_{D=1}\left(D-1\right)^{}}e^{x}=\frac13\cdot xe^{-x} \end{aligned} $$

example2)

$$y''-4y'+3y=-2e^{3x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D-1\right)\left(D-3\right)y_{p}=-2e^{3x} \\ \\ &y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left.\left(D-1\right)\right|_{D=3}\left(D-3\right)}-2e^{3x}=\frac12\cdot x\cdot-2e^{3x} \end{aligned} $$

example3)

$$x^2y''-4xy'+6y=2e^{3x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D-3\right)\left(D-2\right)y_{p}=2e^{3x} \\ \\ &y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D-2\right)\left|_{D=3}^{}\right.\left(D-3\right)^{}}2e^{3x}=2xe^{3x} \end{aligned} $$

example4)

$$x^2y''-4xy'-14y=3e^{-2x}$$
sol

$$ \begin{aligned} &\left(D+2\right)\left(D-7\right)y_{p}=3e^{-2x} \\ \\ &y_{p}=\frac{1}{\left(D-7\right)\left|_{D=-2}\right.\left(D+2\right)}3e^{-2x}=-\frac{x}{3}e^{-2x} \end{aligned} $$
